2 Service Description

Classroom Pro Downloader is a browser plugin engineered strictly for Google Chrome that enables users to batch-download course assets and reference links from Google Classroom. Its core local features include:

  • Batch-downloading materials, assignments, coursework, and stream announcements.
  • Organizing files locally into a structural ZIP tree by course and coursework title.
  • Assembling external materials and attachments into easy-to-use internet shortcuts (`.url` files).
  • Enabling "Master Archive Mode" to scan and compile materials from all your classes simultaneously.
  • Providing Sync-From-Date and file extension filtering tools.
100% Client-Side Engine: The Service operates under a serverless, local architecture. All processing, organization, and packaging steps occur inside your local system memory. No user files, access tokens, or course materials are ever transmitted to the Developer or any external database.

4 Google Account and OAuth Permissions

To identify your enrolled classes and materials, the Extension uses Google's standard OAuth 2.0 authorization framework. By granting access, you permit the Extension to access the following specific, read-only Google API scopes on your behalf:

  • classroom.courses.readonly — to list your active enrolled classes.
  • classroom.coursework.me.readonly — to scan assignments and tasks posted to you.
  • classroom.coursework.students.readonly — to identify coursework attachments visible to students.
  • classroom.courseworkmaterials.readonly — to read class materials posted to your course pages.
  • classroom.announcements.readonly — to access class announcements containing references you wish to download.
Zero Google Drive Permissions: In accordance with our security-first design, Classroom Pro Downloader requests **zero Google Drive scopes**. File downloads are handled locally inside your browser context using session cookie-authenticated background worker fetches, keeping your Drive fully private.

You can manage or completely revoke this connection at any time in your Google Settings panel at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
