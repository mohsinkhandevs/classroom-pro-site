Google API Compliance

Transparent, Least-Privilege Data Access

We design with privacy by default. Classroom Pro Downloader operates under a strict subset of read-only permissions to protect your personal files.

🔑

Secure Sign-In

Authentication is handled locally using Google's official OAuth 2.0 Identity framework. Your password never touches our extension, and credentials remain sandboxed by your browser.

🏫

Read-Only Classroom Scopes

We request access strictly to read enrolled courses, stream materials, and coursework attachments. The extension cannot edit, add, or alter any of your classroom content.

No Google Drive Permissions

Traditional tools request access to read or edit your entire Google Drive. Classroom Pro Downloader requests **zero Google Drive scopes**, instead fetching files locally via direct cookie-authenticated browser session calls.

Serverless Security

A Radical Approach to Privacy

We believe in total offline transparency: we collect absolutely nothing. No cookies, no telemetry, no tracking scripts, and no crash reports. The extension is completely serverless — it communicates directly from your browser to Google APIs and saves bundles directly to your Downloads folder.
