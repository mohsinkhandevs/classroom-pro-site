1 Frequently Asked Questions

Why are my scans or downloads failing?

In stable conditions, Classroom Pro Downloader compiles attachments in seconds. Uptime issues can arise due to high Google Classroom API server loads or brief local connection drops. The Extension includes a smart self-healing retry engine that handles minor packet drops automatically. If a specific folder compilation stalls, keep the browser tab open to let the retry engine finish. Ensure your network is active.

Does the Extension work on all browsers?

Classroom Pro Downloader is currently fully supported on Google Chrome. Support for Microsoft Edge and other Chromium-based platforms is coming soon.

Does Classroom Pro Downloader require Google Drive permissions?

Absolutely not. To deliver a gold standard of personal privacy, Classroom Pro Downloader requests **zero Google Drive permission scopes** (such as drive.readonly). Rather than reading your entire private Drive, it downloads course attachments in the background via secure, cookie-authenticated browser session requests. This sandboxed setup means your Google Drive files remain completely private and isolated.

Need detailed insights regarding security or OAuth credentials? Check out our Privacy Policy to see our dedicated client-side design. We collect absolutely zero personal user data.

How does Master Archive Mode operate?

When you launch the Extension from your main Google Classroom course dashboard, it enters Master Archive Mode. This allows the scanner to discover, check, and batch-download files from all your active enrolled courses simultaneously into a single, structured master ZIP archive.
