1 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why are my downloads failing?

Sometimes Google Drive API limits download rates. The extension has a built-in smart retry mechanism. Leave the tab open and it will automatically retry. If the problem persists, ensure your internet connection is stable.

Does this work on all browsers?

Classroom Pro Downloader works on Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Brave, and other Chromium-based browsers that support Manifest V3.

Why do you need Google Drive permission?

We use the drive.readonly permission solely to download the binary files directly to your device without routing them through third-party servers. We use drive.file exclusively to temporarily convert uploaded Office documents to PDF.
